How Nova Researches a Stock

Modeled on the Standard & Poor's equity-research process. Two AI engines — a quant analyst and a debating research pod — cover the analytical work a human team does.
~43%
of a full human analyst's job automated end-to-end
~51%
of the work software can actually do (the rest needs human relationships with management)

⚙️ Signals — the quant analyst

10 deterministic agents crunch the numbers: valuation (DCF & 3 more methods), fundamentals, growth, technicals, sentiment. Same inputs always give the same answer — auditable and reproducible.

💬 Debate — the research pod

An AI team that reasons like analysts: a lead splits the question, 4 specialists research filings & the web, a skeptic attacks the thesis, and a judge writes the cited verdict.

Understand the company
BUILT
Step 1
Read all public filings

Pulls real SEC 10-K/10-Q filings, finds the relevant passages, and cites them. Missing: earnings-call transcripts.

HUMAN-ONLY
Steps 2 & 4
Talk to management

Calling executives and validating forecasts with them is a human relationship — outside what any software can do.

PARTIAL
Steps 3, 5 & 6
Model earnings & competition

Projects cash flows and sizes up competition/regulation. Missing: a clean 3-yr EPS forecast, structured competitive analysis, accounting clean-up.

Value it & make the call
BUILT
Step 7
Run the valuation

A proper multi-method DCF (4 valuation models, bear/base/bull scenarios). Missing: peer comparison & sum-of-parts.

BUILT
Step 8
Set value & price target

Now sets an explicit 12-month price target (fair value carried forward at the cost of equity) with upside %, plus a risk-adjusted size.

BUILT
Step 9
Make the recommendation

Issues a 1–5 STARS rating (Strong Buy … Strong Sell) plus a Buy/Sell/Hold call with confidence — direct parity with S&P's STARS.

Review & keep watching
PARTIAL — & deeper
Step 10
Challenge & review the thesis

A built-in skeptic attacks the case and a judge weighs the evidence — richer than S&P's chart even shows. Missing: a compliance sign-off gate.

GAP
Step 11
Re-cover it over time

Every run is saved, but there's no scheduler that re-checks a stock each quarter yet. Next: ongoing-coverage monitoring.

Bottom line: Nova automates the analytical spine of equity research — read the filings, value the company, make a call, and stress-test it with an adversarial debate. What it doesn't do is the human relationship work (calling management) and continuous re-coverage — the natural next investments.
